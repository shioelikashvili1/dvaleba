About Us

"Aversi Pharma" company was founded on November 14, 1994 by Paata Kurtanidze. Immediately after its establishment, the company started importing medicines to Georgia through direct contacts. In 2002, "Avers" created a pharmaceutical enterprise - "Avers-Rational". In 2004, the company launched its eponymous magazine, Aversi, which provides information on health and medicine. In 2009, "Avers" founded the "Alfa" insurance company. In 2011, the company acquired the Konstantin Eristavi National Center of Clinical and Experimental Surgery. On August 23, 2011, the "Medalfa" hospital network was established. Since 2014, the former anti-sepsis center has been taken over by "Aversi" company. In 2014, "Aversis Clinic" opened a nuclear medicine center on the territory of Tbilisi Infectious Disease Hospital.
